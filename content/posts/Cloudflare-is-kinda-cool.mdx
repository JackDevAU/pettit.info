Just a small shill for Cloudflare, which I've been using for a long time... but only recently started "using" it properly... and wow, it's kind of amazing?!

* **Cloudflare Tunnels **- I've found myself using this to test mobile apps against a local API, and it's worked amazingly. I even made a [ngrok style wrapper](https://github.com/JackDevAU/airlock) around it!
* **Cloudflare Workers **- even though it's not "Node," V8 isolates are pretty amazing, and they now power quite a few of my sites.
* **Zero Trust **- woah, not to take a dig at Vercel... but remember that [post I made about paying $150 just to add a password](https://pettit.info/blog/vercel-free-password-protection-nextjs)? Well, Cloudflare's basically like: here's a free tier with OAuth and 50 seats. Super freaking cool.
* **Cloudflare Emails** - want to create a catch-all email alias, or specific email aliases? Yep, Cloudflare can do that too.
* **Cloudflare Domains + DNS control **- I can buy a domain for an actually good price, then do whatever I want with it. Add subdomains, manage DNS easily, and it all integrates really nicely with Workers. It's just so good.
