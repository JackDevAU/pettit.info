## Technical Highlights

- **TinaCMS content editing:** All key pages and sections are editable through TinaCMS, allowing quick updates to services, copy, and contact information.
- **Next.js foundation:** Built on Next.js for fast, SEO-friendly pages with a straightforward deployment pipeline.
- **Form handling and protection:** Contact forms integrate with Resend for email delivery and Google reCAPTCHA for basic spam protection.

## Outcome

The project delivers a clean, professional online presence that is easy for non-technical users to keep up-to-date without sacrificing performance or flexibility.
